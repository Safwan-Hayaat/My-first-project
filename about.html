About us
This is the official website of the ICT Club at Mirpur Siddhanta High School. Created by and made for the students of the ICT Club. Here, you can see details of the ICT Lab, the name of every Committee Member and General Member, the Teachers and more.


ICT is the future of Bangladesh. But, in a world where fraud and the abuse of technology are only growing, we hope to use ICT to make the world better and create a brighter Bangladesh.


This website was created in January of 2026 for the 2026 ICT Fair at the school by 5 students of class VII (section A).
