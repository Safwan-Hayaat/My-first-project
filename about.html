About our members

In Mirpur Siddhanta High School, each club has 3 teachers and 7 executive committee members. Teachers are a type of convener or leader of a club. They make the main decisions of their respective clubs. On the other hand each club also has an executive committee. Committee members usually hold meetings and make decisions for their club. Members of the committee also may handle smaller problems and task. Such as adding new members, assisting in lab classes etc.

Normal members of the ICT get all the benefits of being in the ICT club. They can attend weekly lab classes, make projects and participate in different events. These are all of the perks of being in the ICT club.

About the Website

This is the official website of the ICT Club at Mirpur Siddhanta High School. Created by and made for the students of the ICT Club. Here, you can see details of the ICT Lab, the name of every Committee Member and General Member, the Teachers and more.


ICT is the future of Bangladesh. But, in a world where fraud and the abuse of technology are only growing, we hope to use ICT to make the world better and create a brighter Bangladesh.


This website was created in January of 2026 for the 2026 ICT Fair at the school by 5 students of class VII (section A).

Why should i join the ICT club?

Unlike other clubs, the ICT club is active all year. One of the most popular activities of the ICT club is its lab classes. Most students wish to spend more time in the ICT lab but they usually dont get many classes in the lab each year. The ICT club solves that with lab classes. Each week on Wednesday at 11:00 AM, the ICT club hosts hour long classes in the computer lab. All members of the ICT club are free to participate in classes.

We also host participate in school wide events like the Youth Fair. The Youth Fair is an annual competition to see which club can create the best projects. Because of our extra activities and year round events. You should join the ICT club.

How do i join the ICT club?

If you want to join the ICT club, there are 2 main ways to do so, which are the following,

1. The simplest way to join the ICT club is to go to one of the teachers of the ICT club or a committee member and tell them that you want to join the ICT club.

2. The easier but more complicated way to join the ICT club is on this website. If you are on mobile, then look at the top right of the screen and click on the symbol that has 3 lines on it. Then you can click on the "join" button. If you are on computer then look at the top right of the screen and click "join". Now you will see a form. Fill it out with all the information required and make sure to write your full name in Bangla.

If you want to join the ICT club whatsapp group then you can write your phone number
